# Business Insights — Sales Analytics Dashboard (2014–2017)

This document presents key findings from the Sales Analytics Dashboard,
built using the Sample Superstore dataset in Power BI.

---

## 1. Phones & Chairs Are the Best Performing Sub-Categories

Phones and Chairs each generated $0.33M in total sales, making them
the top two sub-categories in the dataset. This shows that these
products have consistent and high demand compared to others.

---

## 2. Consumer Segment Has the Highest Sales Volume

The Consumer segment recorded the highest number of items sold,
with over 4,000 units. This indicates that individual customers
contribute more to overall sales than Corporate or Home Office segments.

---

## 3. West and East Regions Perform Better Than Central and South

Looking at regional sales, the West and East regions show higher
sales numbers across all categories. Central and South regions
have lower performance, which could be worth investigating further.

---

## 4. Technology Accounts for a Major Share of Revenue

Technology contributes 32.3% ($742K) of total sales, making it
one of the strongest performing categories. This suggests that
technology products are in high demand among customers.

---

## 5. Top 5 Products Are All High Value Items

|                       Product                              |    Sales   |
|------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier                      | $61,599.82 |
| Fellowes PB500 Electric Punch Plastic Comb Binding Machine | $27,453.38 |
| Cisco TelePresence System EX90 Videoconferencing Unit      | $22,638.48 |
| HON S400 Series Task Chairs for Big and Tall               | $21,870.58 |
| GBC DocuBind TL300 Electric Binding System                 | $19,823.48 |

All top 5 products are office equipment and furniture items with
high unit prices. The Canon Copier alone accounts for $61K in sales,
which is significantly higher than the other products in the list.

---

## 6. Profit Margin Is Low Despite High Sales

Total sales reached $2.30M but total profit was only $286.40K,
resulting in a profit margin of just 12%. This suggests that
costs or discounts may be affecting overall profitability.

---

## 7. Home Office Segment Is the Least Explored

The Home Office segment recorded the lowest sales and item count
among all three segments. This could represent a potential growth
area that has not been fully utilized.
